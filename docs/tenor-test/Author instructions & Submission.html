Author Instructions and Submission


Submission

All paper submissions will be peer-reviewed to ensure quality and suitability. Please upload your submission through the Easy Chair submission page. If needed, you can find a tutorial that explains the submission procedure here. At least one author of each accepted paper is required to be registered for the conference by the author registration deadline.

Format of written documents

Please use the provided LaTeX and Word templates, and follow the guidelines and technical instructions for the preparation of papers described in Springer’s Information for LNCS Authors webpage.

We strongly encourage you to use LaTeX2e for the preparation of your camera-ready submissions. All written documents should be submitted in PDF format.
