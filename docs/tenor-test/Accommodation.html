Accommodation


For your arrival at TENOR2021 in Marseille we recommend you to book your hotels as soon as possible.
We also recommend you to choose a hotel close to the city center, since social evening events will take place in this area.
Halfway between conference location (31 chemin Joseph Aiguier) and concerts place (Friche la Belle de Mai) could also be a good choice.
These two places are spaced 6 kms, which takes around 50' by public transportation.



We here propose a list of hotels that are close to public transports stations. No commercial agreements have been concluded with these hotels.
